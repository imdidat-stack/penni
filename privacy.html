Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Penni ("Penni", "we", "us", or "our") is a personal finance app for iPhone. We built Penni to be private by design: your financial information belongs to you, lives on your device, and — if you choose — in your own private iCloud. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles, how it is used, and the choices you have.

The short version. Penni does not run its own servers for your financial data, does not sell your data, and does not use third‑party advertising or analytics trackers. The transactions, budgets, and balances you enter stay on your device and sync only through your personal iCloud account if you enable it.

1. Information We Handle

Financial information you enter

When you use Penni, you can create wallets and record transactions, budgets, goals, categories, and related notes. This information is stored locally on your device using Apple's on‑device database. It is not transmitted to us.

Account information

Penni offers Sign in with Apple and a guest mode. If you sign in with Apple, we receive the information you agree to share through Apple — typically your name and an email address (which may be an Apple private relay address). We use this only to personalize your profile within the app. If you use guest mode, no account information is collected. You may also add a profile photo, which is stored only on your device.

Purchases and subscriptions

Penni offers an optional subscription, Penni Pro. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. The app receives your subscription status (active or not) from Apple in order to unlock Pro features.

Diagnostics

Penni does not include third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. If you opt in through iOS, Apple may share aggregated, anonymous crash and performance diagnostics with us via App Store Connect; this data is not linked to your identity.

2. How Your Information Is Used

  • To provide the app's core features — recording and displaying your finances.
  • To sync and back up your data through your own iCloud account, if you enable it.
  • To manage and restore your Penni Pro subscription.
  • To personalize your in‑app profile (name and optional photo).

We do not use your information for advertising, and we never sell or rent it.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your financial data is stored on your device. If you turn on iCloud Sync, a copy is stored in your private iCloud database, which is associated with your Apple Account and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not operate our own servers that store your transactions, balances, or budgets, and we cannot access your iCloud data.

4. Apple Services

Penni relies on services provided by Apple to function, including Sign in with Apple, iCloud (CloudKit), and the App Store / StoreKit for purchases. Your use of these services is subject to Apple's terms and privacy policy. We only receive the limited information described above.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for their own marketing. We may disclose information only if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or the public — and only to the extent we actually hold such information, which for your financial data we generally do not.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Because your data lives on your device (and optionally your iCloud), you are in control of it at all times:

  • Use Settings → Delete All Data inside the app to permanently remove your records.
  • Delete the app to remove its on‑device data.
  • Remove synced data from iCloud via your device's iCloud settings.

7. Security

Penni stores your data using Apple's secure on‑device storage, and any sign‑in credentials are kept in the device Keychain. You can enable an optional Face ID / Touch ID app lock for an extra layer of privacy. While no method of storage is completely secure, we rely on Apple's platform security to help protect your information.

8. Children's Privacy

Penni is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can address it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA). Because your financial data is stored on your own device and iCloud, you can exercise most of these rights directly within the app. For any request involving information we hold, contact us using the details below.

10. International Users

Penni is available on the App Store internationally. Where iCloud sync is used, your data is handled by Apple in accordance with its policies and the region associated with your Apple Account.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide notice within the app. Your continued use of Penni after an update means you accept the revised policy.
