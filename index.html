Everything you need

Thoughtful features, quietly powerful

Each screen is designed to give you clarity at a glance — no clutter, no noise.

Add by voicePRO

Say “dinner at Luigi’s, 32 dollars on my card” and AI fills in the amount, category, merchant, and date. You just check it and save.

Scan a receipt

Point your camera at a receipt and Penni reads the total, the store, and the date — right on your device, without sending it anywhere.

Wallets & balances

Track every account, card, and cash stash in one place, with a running total that always adds up.

Gentle budgets

Set soft spending limits by category and let Penni help you stay on track — encouraging, never nagging.

Insights & trendsPRO

See exactly where your money goes with beautiful spending trends and category breakdowns.

Savings goals

Set aside money for what matters and watch your progress grow with satisfying, animated rings.

Warm dark modePRO

A soft, easy-on-the-eyes theme for late-night check-ins — as considered as the light one.

iCloud backup & sync

Your data is safely backed up and kept in sync across your devices — encrypted in your own iCloud. Free for everyone.

Locked with Face ID

Keep your finances behind Face ID or Touch ID, so a glance at your phone never becomes a glance at your money.

Widgets & Live Activities

Put your balance and budgets on the Home Screen, and watch a budget tick along live on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

Any currency

Give each wallet its own currency and pick the one you think in, from the full ISO list — the ones you’d expect are right at the top.

Categories your wayPRO

Rename, recolor, and add your own categories from a library of 60 icons and 16 hand-picked colors, so your money is filed the way you think about it.
